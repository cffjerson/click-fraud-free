Protect your website and advertising campaigns from bots, competitors, and invalid traffic with ClickFraudFree’s real-time click fraud detection and prevention.

== Description ==
Invalid traffic, bots, and malicious users can reach your website through paid ads, organic traffic, and direct visits — wasting your advertising budget and distorting your analytics.

ClickFraudFree helps protect your website and ad campaigns by detecting and blocking fraudulent clicks in real time. Our lightweight WordPress plugin makes installation quick and easy, allowing you to secure your traffic without impacting website performance.

ClickFraudFree monitors and protects traffic from:

* Paid traffic (Google Ads, Meta/Facebook Ads, Microsoft Ads)
* Organic traffic
* Direct traffic

With ClickFraudFree’s advanced fraud detection technology, you can focus on growing your business while we handle fraudulent and invalid traffic behind the scenes.

An active ClickFraudFree account is required to use this plugin.

== Installation ==
1. Install the plugin from the WordPress Plugin Directory or upload it manually.
2. Activate the plugin through the “Plugins” menu in WordPress.
3. Enter your ClickFraudFree Client ID in the plugin settings.
4. Save your settings to enable protection.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**Is there a free trial available?**  
Yes. ClickFraudFree offers a free trial so you can analyze your traffic and identify fraudulent activity before committing.

**Will this plugin slow down my website?**  
No. ClickFraudFree uses a lightweight, asynchronous tracking script designed to have no noticeable impact on page load speed or performance.

**What are the benefits of using ClickFraudFree?**  
ClickFraudFree helps protect your ad spend and website by blocking bots, competitors, and malicious users. This ensures your ads reach real potential customers and your analytics remain accurate.

**What do I need for this plugin to work?**  
You need to create a ClickFraudFree account and obtain a Client ID from clickfraudfree.com. The plugin will not function without an active account.
